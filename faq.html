FAQ

What is a microschool?

A small school that is able to guide individual students to fulfill their potential in small groups with personal relationships, accountability, and many opportunities for daily student leadership. Our small size allows for flexibility, integrated learning, and support for following their passions.



Why a mixed-aged group?

Students working together across age groups can flourish without competition-driven grades. Students have opportunities to learn from and teach each other without the pressure of the same age/ability ranking. The expectation is that all students are at different places developmentally and deserve the same respect, patience, and support through challenges from each other and the guides.
